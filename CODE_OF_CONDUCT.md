# Code of Conduct

Crupier is intended for public, professional collaboration. Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep discussions respectful, technical, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and kind.
- Assume good intent while asking for evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on the code, documentation, tests, release process, or user impact.
- Respect privacy. Do not post API keys, prompts, customer data, logs with secrets, or private provider responses in issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Clearly label real-provider results and redact account-specific details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting credentials or sensitive production data.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or block users when behavior harms the project or its users. Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
